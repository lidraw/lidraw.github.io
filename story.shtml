Emu Otori Emu is all happiness and innocence. She acts upon her ideas without a second thought, sometimes to the consternation of her friends. She's searching for like-minded performers to help her bring a deserted stage in Phoenix Wonderland back to its former glory. Tsukasa Tenma Tsukasa has bucketloads of confidence and loves to be the center of attention, although he easily gets carried away. A theater show he saw as a kid impressed him so much that he made it his ultimate goal to become the greatest star in the world. He's a supportive brother to his frail sister, Saki. Rui Kamishiro Nene's neighbor and childhood friend, Rui is an easy-going genius. He's meticulous about making every show the best it can be, making use of his flair for technology and inventiveness. He even builds robots to use on stage. His somewhat unique way of thinking often gets him labeled as a weirdo. Nene Kusanagi Nene joined the group at Rui's recommendation. She's sharp-tongued, but honey-voiced, with the singing ability of a budding musical star. For some reason, she refuses to show herself to Tsukasa and Emu, deploying Robo-Nene to perform shows in her place.
